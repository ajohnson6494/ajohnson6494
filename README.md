# Andrew Johnson

Senior Systems Developer and HIPAA Security Officer at [1 Natural Way](https://github.com/1naturalway), where I've been since interning in 2014. BGSU grad, Whitmer High School alum, and Toledo-based.

I architect and maintain a multi-application Laravel platform that powers insurance-driven medical device fulfillment — spanning ecommerce, internal operations, compliance workflows, and third-party integrations. Most of my work lives on a private GitLab instance; public packages are under [@1naturalway](https://github.com/1naturalway).

Outside of work, I build things for my community, play Minecraft and Cities: Skylines II, collect Star Wars Lego sets, and run a self-hosted Jellyfin library.

## What I work on

- **Multi-app Laravel architecture** - Shared packages, consistent patterns, and reusable abstractions across four interconnected applications
- **Healthcare & compliance systems** - HIPAA-aware workflows for prescriptions, insurance eligibility, document handling, and order fulfillment
- **Infrastructure & performance** - AWS (EC2, S3, CloudFront), Redis caching, PostgreSQL optimization, Core Web Vitals
- **SEO-driven engineering** - Server-rendered architecture, structured data, crawlability, and technical SEO at scale
- **Developer tooling & process** - UAT environments, release management, vendor governance, and internal tooling to reduce operational friction
